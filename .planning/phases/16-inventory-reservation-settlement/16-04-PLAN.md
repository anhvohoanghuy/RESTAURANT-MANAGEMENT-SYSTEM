<objective>
Implement the settlement business logic: the WR-01-safe idempotency-ledger writer and the InventoryReservationSettlementService that converts a held reservation into an actual stock deduction for one order line. This is the core of the phase (D-02..D-06).

Purpose: React to a settle-trigger by re-resolving the line's recipe, locking correctly, subtracting reserved+on_hand with a non-negative clamp, writing a CONSUMPTION audit movement, guarding idempotency two ways, and flipping the reservation to SETTLED on the last line.
Output: InventoryLedgerWriter, InventoryReservationSettlementService, and their tests.
</objective>
