<objective>
Close IN-02: `outbox_events` rows in `SENT` state are never pruned (unbounded table growth slows
`claimPending`), and `FAILED` rows die silently after a single `log.error`. Add a scheduled retention
job that deletes `SENT` rows older than a configurable window and a lightweight surfacing of `FAILED`
rows (scheduled count + `log.warn`/metric) so a poison message is visible to operators.

Purpose: Keep the hot outbox table small and make stuck/poison rows observable.
Output: Repository retention + FAILED-count queries, a property-gated `OutboxRetentionJob`, and tests.
</objective>
