<objective>
Close the Phase 17.1 residual idempotency risk in the Phase 16 inventory settlement consumer.

The service currently inserts the processed-event ledger row through `InventoryLedgerWriter`
in a `REQUIRES_NEW` transaction before the settlement work. If settlement later fails, the ledger
row remains committed and Kafka redelivery skips the event permanently. Move settlement ledger
recording to the end of the same transaction, matching the Phase 17.1 CR-01 fix pattern.
</objective>
