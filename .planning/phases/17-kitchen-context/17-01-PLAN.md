Extend `order_context` so that a CONFIRMED order publishes a new self-contained `OrderConfirmedEvent`

kitchen→order projection will later apply. This is the producer half of D-01 plus the enum half of D-04.

order status needs the vocabulary to reflect fulfillment.

publish call inside `OrderConfirmationService`, four new `OrderStatus` values, and extended unit tests.
