<objective>
Harden the kitchen consumer/aggregate: persist an advance audit trail (K-WR-02), absorb
same-order duplicate OrderConfirmed instead of DLT-ing (K-IN-01/02), and isolate the
ledger insert in a REQUIRES_NEW writer (I-WR-01, kitchen side).

Purpose: actorId is accepted by advance() but never persisted (no accountability of who
advanced an item); a second OrderConfirmed for the same order under a fresh eventId
passes the eventId ledger check then hits the unique orderId constraint on save(ticket)
and lands on the DLT; and the inline saveAndFlush+catch marks the ticket-creation
transaction rollback-only on a concurrent duplicate.
Output: advancedBy/advancedAt columns; existsByOrderId dedup; KitchenLedgerWriter bean.
</objective>
