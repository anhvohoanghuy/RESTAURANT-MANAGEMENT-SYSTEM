<objective>
Cut the order-context saga events over to the transactional outbox (I-WR-02, order
side), adopt the REQUIRES_NEW OrderLedgerWriter in OrderConfirmationService (I-WR-01),
and fix the rejection_reason overflow (I-WR-04).

Purpose: OrderCreated and OrderConfirmed are currently published via afterCommit — a
crash between commit and send strands the order. Writing them to the outbox in the same
transaction closes the gap; the relay (plan 05) publishes them. The inline
saveAndFlush+catch ledger idiom is replaced by the REQUIRES_NEW writer created in plan
03. rejection_reason defaults to VARCHAR(255) and describe() concatenates unbounded
shortfalls — a multi-ingredient rejection risks a DataIntegrityViolationException.
Output: both order events routed through the outbox; ledger isolated; rejection_reason
widened + truncated.
</objective>
