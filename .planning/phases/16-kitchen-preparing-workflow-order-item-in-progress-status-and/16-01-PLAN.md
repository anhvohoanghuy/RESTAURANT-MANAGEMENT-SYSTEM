<objective>
Establish the Order Context domain foundation for the kitchen preparing workflow: add the order-level `OrderStatus.PREPARING` (D-06), a new order-line-level `OrderLineStatus` enum and status column on `OrderLineEntity` (D-01), the `OrderLinePreparingEvent` contract that carries the per-line recipe payload plus `totalLines` for last-line detection, and the new `OrderDomainException` codes the staff endpoint will throw (D-02 guards).

Purpose: These are the contracts and model changes both the order-side service (16-03) and the inventory settlement service (16-04) depend on. Isolated in Wave 1 so both downstream sides can build against a stable event/enum surface in parallel.
Output: `OrderStatus` (+PREPARING), `OrderLineStatus` (new), `OrderLineEntity.status`, `OrderLinePreparingEvent`, `OrderDomainException` (new codes).
</objective>
